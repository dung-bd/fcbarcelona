CAMP NOU

BARCELONA - SPAIN

FC BARCELONA

Més que un club

Enjoy one, two or three weeks of the same kind of life as that lived by the FC Barcelona stars during the rest of the year. At the daily training sessions, you will feel just like one of the Barça first team as you play on the same fields as they do at the Ciutat Esportiva Joan Gamper, known all around the world as La Masía. You will also get to spend your free time with other young boys and girls from around the world who share the same love for soccer, plus all the different cultural and leisure options on offer in such a cosmopolitan and historic city as Barcelona
